Code of Conduct - TampaBay.Gay

TampaBay.Gay is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all users, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, physical appearance, body size, race, age, or religion. We believe in the dignity and the rights of every individual, and we strive to create an online space where everyone feels welcomed, valued, and respected.
By using our website, you agree to adhere to this Code of Conduct:

Respect for all:Every user is expected to interact with respect towards each other. Discrimination, bigotry, or hateful language in any form is unacceptable and will not be tolerated.

No harassment:Harassment in any form, including offensive comments, unwelcome sexual attention, stalking, or abusive language, will not be tolerated.

Relevant Content: All content shared or published on this website should be travel-related and relevant to our mission of providing helpful travel information for everyone, especially for our LGBTQ+ community.

Privacy: Respect the privacy of others. Never reveal someone elseâ€™s personal information without their explicit permission. This includes sharing private communications without consent.

Commercial Conduct:Unethical or unsolicited commercial conduct, including spamming or overly promotional content, is not permitted.

Legal: Users should comply with all applicable laws and regulations. Illegal activity will not be tolerated on this website.

Responsible Use:Users should not post or transmit content that contains viruses, worms, malicious code, or any software intended to damage or alter a computer system or data.

Violations of this code of conduct may lead to temporary or permanent ban from the website. We reserve the right to remove any content or terminate any accounts that violate this code. If you experience or witness any form of misconduct or have any concerns about a violation of this code, please contact us immediately at sean@tampabay.gay. This Code of Conduct may be revised at any time and the use of this website is subject to the most recent version. We are excited to have you in our community and we look forward to providing you with a safe, respectful, and friendly experience.
